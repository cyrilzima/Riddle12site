Privacy Policy for Stardust Gems

Effective Date: December 4, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Kirill Petrov (“I”, “me”, or “my”) handles user information in the mobile game Stardust Gems (“the App”). I respect your privacy and am committed to being transparent about how information is handled.

⸻

2. Personal Information

The App itself does not require users to provide direct personal information such as name, address, email, phone number, photos, or account registration. However, certain technical and usage data may be collected automatically through third-party services as described in Section 3 of this Policy.

⸻

3. Third-Party Services & Data Collection

The App uses third-party services that may collect certain information for advertising, analytics, and performance measurement purposes. This may include device identifiers and data related to in-app activity such as game progress and purchases.

These third-party services operate under their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob – Used to display advertisements and measure ad performance.
    Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple Game Center – Used to provide achievements and leaderboards.
    Apple Privacy Policy
  • Meta (Facebook) SDK – Used for advertising analytics and attribution.
    Meta Privacy Policy
  • TikTok Business SDK – Used to measure advertising performance.
    TikTok Privacy Policy
  • Firebase (Google Analytics & Crash Reporting) – Used to collect anonymized analytics data and crash reports to improve app stability and performance.
    Firebase Privacy Policy

If you grant permission via the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt, some of these services may use your device identifier to provide personalized advertising and measure ad effectiveness. You can change or withdraw this permission at any time in your iOS system settings.

⸻

4. Children’s Privacy

The App is suitable for a general audience. The App itself does not require users to provide any personal information.

Personalized advertising is only used with the user’s permission via Apple’s App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. If permission is not granted, only non-personalized ads may be shown.

Some third-party services used in the App may collect limited device or usage data as described in Section 3, in accordance with their own privacy policies.

⸻

5. Data Security

The App does not directly store personal data on its own servers. Any data processed by third-party partners is protected according to their own security standards and privacy policies.

⸻

6. In-App Purchases

The App offers an optional in-app purchase to remove ads. All payments are processed securely through Apple’s App Store. I do not collect or store any payment or billing information.

⸻

7. Your Consent

By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not install or use the App.

⸻

8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal data. You may also withdraw consent for personalized advertising at any time through your iOS device settings.

For any privacy-related requests, including questions about data access or deletion, you may contact me using the information below.
